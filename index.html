The President.





The latest data on unemployment are always headline news together with the rate of inflation. In general sense, stability and prosperity have been among the primary goals of government policy for centuries.

Most people receive bulk of their income in the form of wages and salaries. Wage and salary earners feel that they are badly hurt by inflation. They compare what their paychecks can buy each time at ever-higher prices with what they would be able to buy with the same paychecks if prices remained stable.

With 80% unemployment rate, a budget of $3 billion and 0% inflation rate. Let's see what you can do as the President.
